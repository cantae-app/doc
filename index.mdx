**Cantaê is a desktop music player with karaoke support.**

With the advancement of Artificial Intelligence technology, it has become easier to separate the vocals from your favorite songs. The software manages vocal and playback tracks, controlling volume while automatically detecting synchronized lyrics.

<Warning>Cantaê does not offer music for sale or distribute it illegally, we fully respect copyright and encourage everyone to purchase music from legitimate sources for use in our app.</Warning>
